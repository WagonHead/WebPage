About us

Wagon Head is an indie game studio from Porto Alegre (POA), Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. We are a group of friends building games with effort, chaos, and a lot of heart.

✦

Innovation-first

We prefer to deliver a game with different mechanics rather than stick to the usual formula just to make a profit or follow industry patterns. Making games is our passion, and that comes first.

🚂

High energy

We’re a small team that talks a lot, get thing´s done with a good vibe and really enjoys the 'late-night' work mommentum. We obsess over what feels fun, weird, and memorable, and we bring that energy into every build.

📀

Small but gold

Keep it simple, that´s it, simple, pure, but gold. For now that´s what we are trying to achieve in our games.

Launched

Breakout Striker

In Development

Breakout Strikers is a competitive hybrid of Breakout + Pong, wrapped in a football/soccer theme. The player controls the bottom paddle, while the opponent controls the top paddle. Both start with their own ball and must break blocks to open lanes, create pressure, and eventually score goals behind the enemy paddle.
